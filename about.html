About Ursula Gray

I am a graphic designer with over 10 years of experience in branding and illustration. I have worked primarily with B2B SaaS startups, and in my free time I have created webcomics and taught young artists and writers how to make their own comics.

With a background in marketing and sequential art, I create expressive designs with a heavy focus on storytelling. In my work, I craft narratives through bold illustration, colour, and type, while remaining approachable and playful. I value accessibility, sustainability, and helping those in need. I strive to work with companies that are trying to make the world a better place.
